About Me

Hi all! My name is Nate Johnson and I am from Chicago, Illinois. I grew up in the northwest suburbs of the city and moved downtown for college, where I studied Public Policy with a specialization in Education Policy.

My professional background is mostly in the non-profit sector. My first job out of college was with an organization called College Possible that supported first-generation college students in CPS.

For the last two years I worked at the Greater Chicago Food Depository where I supported the Community Engagement Team in their fight to end hunger in Chicago. I am very excited for this new venture into coding and web development!
